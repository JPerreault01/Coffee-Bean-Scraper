*How each source community prioritizes coffee decisions. Useful when targeting a specific audience.*

**https://perfectdailygrind.com/2024/11/future-for-espresso-machine-technology/**

Perfect Daily Grind frames espresso machine technology through the lens of operational efficiency, barista empowerment, and the ongoing tension between craft identity and automation. It prioritises how technology serves both quality and business sustainability, positioning automation as a pragmatic evolution rather than a threat to specialty coffee values.

**https://perfectdailygrind.com/2021/03/exploring-the-chinese-coffee-supply-chain/**
