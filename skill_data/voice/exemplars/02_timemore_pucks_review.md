TIMEMORE PUCKS ESPRESSO ACCESSORIES SET REVIEW
Source: pullandpourcoffee.com | Author: Andrew Pautler | Date: September 20, 2025

OVERVIEW
The Timemore PUCKS Espresso Accessories Set is a bundled trio of puck prep tools designed to bring consistency to home espresso. The set includes a WDT tool, a self-adjusting distributor, and a calibrated tamper.

WHAT'S IN THE BOX
- Orbital WDT Tool — magnetic base, 8 ultra-fine 0.3mm needles to break up clumps and redistribute grounds evenly, preventing channeling. Comes with replacement needles and an allen wrench.
- Gravity Distributor — snowflake-style self-adjusting base that levels the coffee bed without needing manual depth adjustment. Glides in both directions; snaps magnetically to its holder.
- Calibrated Tamper — applies a consistent 30 lbs of pressure with a gradual "click" mechanism for repeatable tamping shot to shot.

KEY FEATURES
- Ergonomic Design: Wide, rounded grips; WDT tool handle doubles as a magnetic storage stand. Tactile feedback from the tamper click is intuitive even for beginners.
- Build Quality: Durable stainless steel bases, matte finish, reassuring weight. Magnetic integrations keep the prep area tidy.
- Cohesive System: Each tool flows into the next step of puck prep, creating a unified workflow rather than three standalone items.
- Available in black or wood finishes.

WHAT WORKS WELL
- Streamlined workflow from distribution through tamping
- Magnetic design details (storage, holder snap) are practical and satisfying
- Calibrated tamper improves repeatability and reduces channeling
- Visually appealing; consistent with Timemore's design aesthetic
- Consistent extraction results once the workflow is established

SHORTCOMINGS
- Not a new concept — bundled espresso prep kits have existed for years; PUCKS enters a category that peaked in popularity previously
- Price: ~$199 retail ($150 pre-sale); on the expensive side; may be a barrier for beginners or redundant for those who already own similar tools

OVERALL VERDICT
A polished, well-engineered puck prep kit. Best suited for:
- Home baristas just starting out who want to standardize their process
- Experienced users looking to refresh their workflow with a cohesive, quality set

Not ideal for those who already own a WDT tool, distributor, and tamper they're happy with. At $199, the value depends on what you're upgrading from.

Rating: Recommended with caveats on price.
